For marine benthic and some fish species characterized with a sedentary adult phase and a pelagic phase, climate change affects both adult phase as well as pelagic phase and ocean circulations; climate change can decrease demographical fitness during adults phase and behavioral fitness during pelagic phase such as reduce larval duration and shift spawning time. Our aim is to understand the combined effects on adults and pelagic phase on regional stability.
